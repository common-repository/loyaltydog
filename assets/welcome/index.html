Welcome to LoyaltyDog

Thank you for installing LoyaltyDog.

In order for LoyaltyDog to properly function, you need to insure the following:

  • The latest version of Woocommerce is installed and activated, and
  • You have an active LoyaltyDog subscription for the WordPress Plugin at the add-on or standalong level

If you have a subscription and have received your API credentials from LoyaltyDog, then go to Settings to enter this information.

If you do not yet have a LoyaltyDog subscription for WordPress, please go to LoyaltyDog and purchase the add-on or standalong level

For any questions or general support, please visit our support website.
